Diabetes

Diabetes is a disease that affects the ability of your body to use blood glucose (blood sugar). Glucose is very important for your health as it provides energy to the cells (muscles and tissues) of your body and brain.

Suggested yoga poses

Yoga poses

Bharadvajasana I

This is a twisting yoga for diabetes patient and it is very important.

Bharadvajasana II

This is also a twisting asana and a variation of Bhardvajasana I above.

Ardha matsyendrasana

This asana is also a twisting one that activates the pancreas and very much effective

Marichyasana

This asana is a twisting one that activates the pancreas and very much effective by using bricks rather than doing without support.

Navasana

This yogafor diabetes reduces stiffness from the stomach part and as a result, helps the hormonal secretion process.

Supta padangusthasana

This asana improves the blood circulation and in the internal organs of the stomach.
